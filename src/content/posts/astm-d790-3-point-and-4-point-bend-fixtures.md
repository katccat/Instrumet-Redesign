Instru-Met offers a versatile range of 3-point and 4-point bend test fixtures designed to meet ASTM D790 and ISO 178 standards. Our fixtures are compatible with a wide variety of materials, including plastics, ceramics, wood, carbon fiber, and metals.

Each fixture is engineered to perform both 3-point and 4-point flexural tests with interchangeable components. We offer customizable loading noses and support anvils in any diameter to suit your specific testing requirements.

Whether you’re testing rigid plastics or advanced composites, Instru-Met’s precision fixtures deliver repeatable and reliable results.
