Regular calibration is an essential safeguard for the accuracy of tensile testing
machines, which are vital for characterizing the mechanical behavior of
materials.

Instru-Met Corporation holds accreditation for the following:

1. **Force Verification of Testing Machines (Compression / Tension)** — ASTM E4 and ISO 7500-1
2. **Extensometers — Displacement / Strain Measuring Devices, Gage Length and Crosshead Displacement** — ASTM E83, ISO 9513, ASTM E2309
3. **Testing Machine Crosshead Speed** — ASTM E2658

Instru-Met has been serving our customers for over 50 years. Our engineers
provide on-site calibration for various industries like pharmaceuticals,
aerospace, automotive, medical and biomedical, food, plastics, rubber, textiles,
research, and academia.

We also offer in-shop repair services for load cells, extensometers, grips,
fixtures, and parts for universal testing machines.
