Regular calibration is an essential safeguard for the accuracy of tensile
testing machines, which are vital for characterizing the mechanical behavior of
materials. As an A2LA-accredited laboratory in the field of calibration,
Instru-Met performs:

- Force verification of testing machines (compression and tension) to ASTM E4
  and ISO 7500-1
- Extensometer, displacement, and strain measuring devices (gage length and
  crosshead displacement) to ASTM E83, ISO 9513, and ASTM E2309
- Testing machine crosshead speed to ASTM E2658

Instru-Met has served customers for over 50 years. Our engineers provide
on-site calibration for pharmaceuticals, aerospace, automotive, medical and
biomedical, food, plastics, rubber, textiles, research, and academia. We also
offer in-shop repair of load cells, extensometers, grips, fixtures, and parts.
Please contact us with your calibration needs or questions.
