Rated to 2.5 kN (500 lbf), Instru-Met's capstan grips are engineered for
high-accuracy tensile testing of twisted cords, braided lines, elastic tapes,
and other flexible specimens. A self-tightening spiral groove applies even
stress distribution, minimizes slippage, and eliminates jaw breaks.

Key features:

- 500 lbf (2.5 kN) capacity for cords, yarns, and tapes
- Self-tightening capstan design that locks tighter as force increases
- 2.5 full turns of grooved spiral for even stress distribution
- Sample diameters from 0.02 to 0.125 inches (0.5 to 3.2 mm)
- Operating range from -100F to 600F (-73C to 316C)
- Half-inch clevis pin mount compatible with most standard machines
- Optional smooth capstans for delicate or non-abrasive materials
