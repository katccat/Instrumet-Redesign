Is your Instron 5500 tensile testing machine reaching the end of its life? We
can keep it running. This covers systems such as the 5542, 5543, 5544, 5545,
5565, 5567, 5587, and 5588 in phase 4.

1. Phone diagnosis and troubleshooting. A trained service engineer helps
   diagnose the issue over the phone and walks you through basic steps.
2. On-site repairs and calibrations. When needed, we come to your lab with the
   necessary parts and fix the machine on-site. We also offer preventive
   maintenance and accredited calibrations.
3. Parts shipment. Ship us parts for evaluation and repair, or we can ship you
   tested parts from our warehouse for self-replacement.

We are confident we can fix your machine and keep it running for the rest of
its life. Contact us for more information or to schedule service.
