Is your Instron 5500 tensile testing machine reaching the end of its life? Don't
worry — we're here to help you keep it running smoothly. Here are the steps we
can take to assist you:

## 1. Phone Diagnosis and Troubleshooting

First, one of our trained service engineers will help diagnose the issue over the
phone. We'll walk you through basic troubleshooting steps to identify the problem.

## 2. On-Site Repairs and Calibrations

If needed, we can come to your lab with all the necessary parts and fix your
Instron 5500 on-site. Our engineers are equipped to handle most repairs quickly
and efficiently. We also offer preventive maintenance and accredited calibrations
to ensure your machine continues to operate at peak performance.

## 3. Parts Shipment

You can ship us parts for service. We provide comprehensive evaluation and
troubleshooting of components to identify and resolve any issues. Alternatively,
we can ship you tested parts from our warehouse for self-replacement.

We are confident we can fix your machine and keep it running for the rest of its
life.
