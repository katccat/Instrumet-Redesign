Instru-Met’s Hi-X Extensometer adds the capability of testing highly elastic materials such as elastomers, semi-rigid plastics and films to any test frame for any manufacturer. Quick release clamps are ergonomically designed to allow one-handed specimen loading and the gripping force is easily adjusted to apply different degrees of tension to ensure the sample is held securely without damage. We offer repair and replacement for all Instron Extensometers.
