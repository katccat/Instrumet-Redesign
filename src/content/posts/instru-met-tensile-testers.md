and examined countless machines. That experience tells us what fails and why,
which lets us improve the design and performance of our own tensile testers.

With over 50 years of expertise, an in-house machine shop, and strong
partnerships, we tailor, tune, and build our testers to your specification:
mechanically, electrically, and operationally. Our machines carry an
industry-leading 24-month warranty.

no end-of-life policies; we provide repairs, support, and upgrades to keep your
machine at peak performance. In many cases we resolve issues within 24 hours.
You can reuse your existing grips and fixtures, and trade-in upgrades help you
get the most value from your investment.
