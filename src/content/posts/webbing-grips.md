Webbing grips or Capstan fixtures are engineered for tensile testing of straps, ribbons, seat belts, slings and webbing. These extra wide materials are fixed around the wrapping column and then secured by locking pins. The grips are ideal for high strength webbing materials. Call us to design the right fixture for your tensile tester!
