Regular calibration safeguards the accuracy of tensile testing machines. As an
A2LA-accredited laboratory in the field of calibration, Instru-Met provides
on-site force verification (compression and tension) to ASTM E4 and ISO 7500-1,
extensometer and displacement calibration to ASTM E83, ISO 9513, and
ASTM E2309, and crosshead-speed verification to ASTM E2658. We serve
pharmaceuticals, aerospace, automotive, medical, food, plastics, rubber,
textiles, research, and academia.
