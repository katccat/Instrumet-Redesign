Instru-Met rebuilds, retrofits, and services universal test frames across the
range: Model 1000 and 1011 tabletop frames, the 1120 series, the 4500 series,
floor models, and multi-head configurations. Frames can be tuned mechanically,
electrically, and operationally to your specification. You keep your existing
grips and fixtures, which passes real savings on to you.
