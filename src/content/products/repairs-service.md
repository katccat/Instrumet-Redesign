Instru-Met repairs tensile and compression testers on-site and in our
laboratory. We service Instron, MTS, Sintech, Q-Test, Tinius Olsen, and other
OEM frames. Support starts with phone diagnosis, moves to on-site repair with
parts in hand when needed, and includes a parts-shipment option for
self-replacement. There are no end-of-life policies here: we support equipment
for as long as it runs.
